Our Products

Raw Cotton Bale

Short Description:

Premium quality Raw Cotton Bales sourced from trusted Indian farms. Ideal for textile mills and spinning industries. Our bales are carefully pressed, contamination-free, and meet international export standards — ensuring superior yarn quality and smooth processing.

Export & Usage Information:

  • Applications: Textile mills, spinning industries, yarn manufacturers.
  • Export Destinations: Bangladesh, Vietnam, China, Indonesia, Cambodia, and Turkey.
  • Quality Assurance: 100% machine-cleaned, contamination-free, and tested as per international standards.

Packaging & Transportation:

  • Bales are securely packed with iron or plastic straps.
  • Wrapped with HDPE or cotton cloth for safe handling during export.
  • Container loading: approx. 110–120 bales per 40ft container.
