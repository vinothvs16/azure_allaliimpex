Cotton Bale Description

Cotton bales are compressed bundles of raw cotton fiber, carefully processed and packed for safe storage, transportation, and export. They are the standard form in which cotton is traded globally and supplied to spinning mills and textile manufacturers. At Allali Impex Private Limited, we supply high-quality cotton bales sourced from trusted Indian ginning mills. Our cotton is processed under strict quality control to ensure uniform fiber length, strength, cleanliness, and low contamination.

Each cotton bale is:

  • Machine-pressed and securely packed
  • Wrapped with export-grade packing material
  • Clearly marked for traceability
  • Compliant with international export standards
  • Key Features of Our Cotton Bales
  • Natural, clean, and contamination-free cotton
  • Consistent fiber quality suitable for spinning
  • Proper moisture control to prevent damage
  • Strong and safe packaging for sea shipments

Applications

Our cotton bales are widely used in:

  • Spinning mills
  • Yarn manufacturing
  • Textile and garment industries
  • Quality & Compliance

We ensure that every shipment meets buyer specifications and is supported with necessary documentation such as:

  • Bale weight and specification details
  • Quality test reports (if required)
  • Phytosanitary and export certificates
  • With a strong focus on quality, transparency, and timely delivery, Allali Impex Private Limited is committed to being a reliable cotton bale exporter to international markets.
